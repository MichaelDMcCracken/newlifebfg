Hosea 8–10

God's Indictment and Israel's Perceived Innocence

Big Idea

Hosea continues to list out the sins of Israel as the reasons why Assyria is going to come and bring judgment upon them.

God's Case Against Israel:

  • Israel rebelled against the Law.
  • Israel didn't know the Lord.
  • Israel spurned the good.
  • Israel made idols.
  • Israel hired allies in Egypt and Assyria.
  • Israel erected altars.
  • Israel worshipped Ba'al.
  • Israel had sexual sin.
  • Israel persecuted the prophets.
  • Israel corrupted themselves.
  • Israel trusted themselves.
